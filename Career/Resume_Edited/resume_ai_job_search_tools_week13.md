# Resume Clinic Week 13: AI Job Search Tools (Informal Session)
Source: https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk
Date: February 4, 2026

> **Note:** This was an informal session with only one attendee (Jonathan/Turtle). Chelsea postponed the formal Week 13 content (Resume and LinkedIn Alignment Audit) to the following week. The conversation focused on Jonathan's AI-powered job search workflow.

---

## [0:00](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=0) [approx] MCP Toolkit for LinkedIn Job Search

- Jonathan set up an **MCP (Model Context Protocol) toolkit** integrated with LinkedIn for automated job searching.
- LinkedIn initially blocked the bot, but he reconfigured it to **pace its requests** so it doesn't trigger rate-limiting.
- The tool **searched 19 jobs**, then automatically **wrote cover letters and three different resume versions** tailored to each role.

---

## [1:50](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=110) [approx] Applying at Scale with AI Assistance

- Applied to **12 different positions in one afternoon** (approximately 3 hours).
- Still had to manually fill out each application, but the AI handled the searching, cover letter drafting, and resume tailoring — making the process **significantly faster** than doing everything manually.
- **3 of the 12 applications** were specifically for **AI/LLM roles**. For those, Jonathan mentioned in the cover letter that he found the position through AI and drafted the letter with AI assistance.

### [9:03](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=543) Human Review Step

- Jonathan **does not let AI send applications automatically**. He proofreads every cover letter before submission.
- This keeps a human-in-the-loop quality check on all outgoing materials.

---

## [9:50](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=590) MoltBot: Async Background Automation

- **MoltBot** is a new tool (originally called "Claudebot," renamed due to copyright concerns).
- It runs **asynchronously in the background**, handling **cron jobs** and long-running tasks.
- Jonathan used MoltBot to power the job search workflow described above.

### [10:07](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=607) MoltBot as Twitch Co-Host Chatbot

- Jonathan set MoltBot up as a **chatbot on his Twitch channel** to handle viewer engagement.
- Previously, he would miss chat messages while coding on stream. Viewers would ask questions like "What language are you using?" and leave before he noticed.
- MoltBot now **responds to chat in real time**, increasing viewer interaction and potential subscriber retention.

---

## [10:37](https://youtu.be/U0gHr-nyqsk?t=637) Automated Twitch Channel with AI-Generated Content

- To run MoltBot as a chatbot, Jonathan had to register it as its own Twitch channel. He then decided to make it a **fully automated 24/7 channel**.
- He is using **Remotion** (a React-based framework) to generate animated video content programmatically.
- The content source is **Docker transcripts** he had previously pulled — the same transcript-extraction workflow used for the career coaching series.
- MoltBot creates videos from those transcripts and streams them on the automated channel continuously.
- Jonathan's personal channel has about **204 followers**; he is curious how long it takes the automated channel to surpass that.
