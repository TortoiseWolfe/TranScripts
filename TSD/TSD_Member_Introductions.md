**TSD Member Introductions on Social Media**

**Video Title Format**

```
Meet TechStack Devs: [Full Name] | [2-3 Highlights]
```

Example: `Meet TechStack Devs: Jonathan Pohlner | Revit Plugins, SpokeToWork & AI Bots`

Pick 2-3 highlights that showcase the member's specialization, projects, or unique angle.

**Suggested Flow (3–5 minutes)**

1. Why TSD — Why you joined, what you’re excited about  
2. What do you specialize in?  
3. Work — A past or current project where you solved a real problem (doesn’t have to be a TSD client)  
4. Other stuff — Book, piano, LLC, community, hobby, etc.  
5. Where to find you — Link/contact if they want to share it

**Bullet Points for Members (pre-interview)**

Before filming, each member should prepare:

* 1–2 sentences on why you joined TSD — e.g. "I wanted to be part of a team without giving up independence" or "I liked the idea of working with people I trust on bigger projects."  
* 1-2 things you feel especially qualified for \- the type of work people come to you for.  
* One project you’ve worked on (past or current) — Who it was for, what problem it solved, and what you did technically or personally.  
* One thing they’re doing outside TSD — Book, piano teaching, LLC, community, etc., and what they want people to know.  
* Where people can find them — LinkedIn, site, book, etc. (optional)

**Interview Script**

Opening  
\> "Today I’m talking with \[Name\], one of our co-founders at Tech Stack Devs. \[Name\], thanks for jumping on."

1\. Why TSD  
\> "Why did you join Tech Stack Devs, and what are you most excited about with what we’re building?"

2\. Specialization  
\> What do you specialize in?  What do people come to you for?

3\. Work  
\> "Tell us about a project you’ve worked on — maybe something you’re doing now or something recent. What problem were you solving, and what did you build?"

4\. Other stuff  
\> "You’re also doing \[book / piano / LLC / community\] — tell us about that and why it matters to you."

5\. Wrap-up  
\> "Where can people find you or learn more about what you’re up to?"

Closing  
\> "Thanks, \[Name\]. This is the kind of talent we have at Tech Stack Devs — people building real things and doing interesting work outside of code too."
